I saw these purple flowers today:

so Purple that—

The kind of purple you just want to Lick,

Or maybe climb inside and

Stroke against your skin,

Like rolling in new spring grass

when you’re taking that winter sweater off

for the first time and

can feel the wind sliding into your

soft Mammal arm hairs, and

if it were sunny, and didn’t rain yesterday, and if

New spring grass were purple

—I just had to share them with someone.
