Cookware with Purpose

Welcome to this week’s edition of Basketball Shoes. It’s an honor to be a part of this community. I’ve got a weekend project that I am excited to share with you.

Do you have an old, busted cast iron skillet? Do you have no cast iron skillet at all? I’ve put together this video to show you how to restore cast iron cookware to pristine condition for about $20.

You will need:

  • Cast iron cookware (I bought this rusted one on Facebook Marketplace for $12)
  • White vinegar ($3)
  • Steel wool ($3)
  • 100% Cold Pressed, Filtered Flaxseed Oil ($9)

This process requires about 1 hour of active time spread out over 24 hours, so plan accordingly.

My intention in sharing this is twofold:

  1. With these instructions, I hope that you can help a cast iron find new purpose in its life.
  2. I hope that this amatuer video inspires you to create something.
