I found this poem in my Notes app that I jotted down on my way to work the morning of 8/28/18. Publishing it here is an ode to the nostalgia I've been feeling generally in the pandemic and recently for summer to return.


Note From The Past

Beautiful summer morning in New York. Moon still in the sky while sunrise paints the city a sweet pink. I can smell the remains of a cooler evening and I feel the descending humidity that will soon envelop us all.

Abby Yee
